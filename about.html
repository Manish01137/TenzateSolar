Our Story

Lighting India Since 2008

TRENZ LITE® is a prominent brand of Ganpati Industry based in Rajpura, Punjab, India. Ganpati Industry has been serving its clients since 2008. We are known as the foremost brand & manufacturer of an exclusive range of LED lighting products like LED Lamp, LED COB Lights, Concealed Lights, Panel Lights, Flood Lights, Architecture Lights & many more — not only made in India, but at very competitive prices.

We have constructed a well-developed and sophisticated in-house infrastructure that enables us to provide A-to-Z solutions, including mould-making facilities. Our unit is well-resourced with all requisite tools and machines divided into various sub-functional units. Our prime motto is to give the best quality products and service to our customers.
